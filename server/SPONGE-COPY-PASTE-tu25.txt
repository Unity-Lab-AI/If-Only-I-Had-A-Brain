WHAT'S IN IT (all server-side — server/brain-server.js, gpu.js, state.js, chat.js)
  A  FLOOD SHED — the bound-Hebbian teach batch DROPS instantly when the target
     socket buffers > DREAM_WS_SOFT_SHED_MB (default 64MB). Safe by design: the
     CPU CSR is authoritative, the GPU shadow re-converges via the TU.20.2
     auto-resync once the buffer drains. New counter: wsPressure.sheds.
  B  HONEST HEARTBEAT — ping misses are FORGIVEN while our send buffer to that
     socket > DREAM_HB_BUF_FORGIVE_MB (default 32MB): the ping physically never
     left the box, so the miss is our congestion, not donor death.
  C  CANONICAL-UPLOAD GRACE — a socket that had a chunked upload dispatched to
     it < 90s ago is busy receiving the brain; the primary's initGpu canonical
     upload finally gets the same grace replica-syncs had (HBGRACE.3 gap).
     The hard ceiling is unchanged — a truly dead socket still dies.
  D  CHURN BRAKES — promote-stronger DEFERS while the current primary is
     mid-canonical-upload or within DREAM_DF7_PROMOTE_COOLDOWN_S (default 120s)
     of the last promotion (deferred newcomers join as replicas; the periodic
     rebalance promotes them once proven). And all in-flight sparse reqIds are
     tagged with their target socket + CANCELLED the moment that donor
     disconnects — no more 180s-timeout storms after a churn kill.
  E  wMAX RESTORE INTEGRITY — _loadBinaryWeights now inherits the construction-
     time wMin/wMax clamps onto restored matrices (the "proj.wMax non-finite
     (Infinity)" WARN class — iter14-D — is dead at the root).
  G  MIND'S EYE (Gee directive) — she images what she's THINKING: field C seeds
     from her latest inner-thought/emission embedding with a rotating 70/30
     older-thought blend (experimentation), or her sem-region activation before
     any thoughts exist — never the raw whole-cortex neuron map. The viewer's
     "source" now reads thought / thought-blend / sem-state.
